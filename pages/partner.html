Frequently Answer & Questions

What is dot-dukaan?
DotDukaan is a B2B e-commerce platform dedicated to transforming the online shopping experience in Pakistan. We connect buyers with verified suppliers, providing access to a wide range of quality products at competitive prices while ensuring seamless transactions and efficient logistics.
Who can use DotDukaan?
DotDukaan caters to retailers, wholesalers, drop shippers, e-commerce store owners, and businesses looking to source products in bulk from verified suppliers.
 How does DotDukaan work?
Buyers can browse products, compare prices, place bulk orders, and connect with suppliers. Vendors can list their products, receive orders, and expand their market reach through our platform.
Why choose DotDukaan?
  • Comprehensive Product Availability: Products from top manufacturers, retailers, and wholesalers are available in one place.
  • Verified Sellers: Only verified sellers list products, ensuring authenticity and high-quality standards.
  • Competitive Prices & High-Quality Products: Businesses can access reasonably priced, high-quality products, reducing return risks and enhancing satisfaction.
